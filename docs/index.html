Kalita wallet

Kalita is a mobile and desktop wallet for Ergo platform, which combines high privacy and friendly UX.

Download

Open-source MIT License. v0.1.0

Security

We build wallet with credo "Not your keys, not your coins". Private keys are kept in encrypted storage locally on your device.

Privacy

No public keys are sent to server to prevent surveillance. Tor feature will hide your network address.

Use everywhere

Manage your coins with mobile Android/iOS and with desktop Windows/Linux/MacOS.

Atomic Swap

Exchange coins without third party with onchain Atomic Swaps.

Mixer

Scrumble public history of coins with Ergo Mixer integration.

Tokens

Manage SigmaUSD and other Ergo tokens

Roadmap

Raising funds

Q3 2021

Description of design choices. Contact with community and adjusting features for it demand. Collection of funds for a full time occupation for the wallet. Failure of fund raising won't stop developing, but will slow down significantly.

Basic wallet

Q4 2021

Basic wallet that can send/receive Ergo in Android devices. Encryption with Android Keystore system.

Tokens

Q1 2022

Support for SigmaUSD and other tokens of user choice.

iOS

Q2 2022

iOS adaptation and publication in Apple App store.

Desktop

Q3 2022

Add desktop platform (Windows, MacOS, Linux), encryption of keys storage on disk, packaging for target platforms.

Mix

Q4 2022

Integration of Ergo Mixer into wallet, design restore system for mix results.

Bitcoin

Q1 2023

Basic bitcoin wallet for atomic swap purposes.

Atomic swap

Q2-Q4 2023

Bitcoin-Ergo atomic swap with swap requests desk on wallet backend side. Addition of tokens swapping.
