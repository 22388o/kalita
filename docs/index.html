Kalita wallet

Kalita is a mobile and desktop wallet for Ergo platform, which combines high privacy and friendly UX.

Download

Open-source MIT License. v0.1.0

Security

We build wallet with credo "Not your keys, not your coins". Private keys are kept in encrypted storage locally on your device.

Privacy

No public keys are sent to server to prevent surveillance. Tor feature will hide your network address.

Use everywhere

Manage your coins with mobile Android/iOS and with desktop Windows/Linux/MacOS.

Atomic Swap

Exchange coins without third party with onchain Atomic Swaps.

Mixer

Scrumble public history of coins with Ergo Mixer integration.

Tokens

Manage SigmaUSD and other Ergo tokens
